HOGWARTS SCHOOL OF WITCHCRAFT AND WIZARDRY

HOGWARTS SCHOOL OF WITCHCRAFT AND WIZARDRY is a fictional boarding school for young witches and wizards in J.K. Rowling's Harry Potter series. Founded over a thousand years ago by the four greatest witches and wizards of the age—Godric Gryffindor, Helga Hufflepuff, Rowena Ravenclaw, and Salazar Slytherin—Hogwarts is located in a remote area of Scotland, hidden from the non-magical world by powerful enchantments. The school serves as the primary setting for much of the series, where young witches and wizards receive their magical education and training.
